# Bellbound Academy Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Bellbound Academy is a single-player school simulation game. This privacy policy explains what information the game uses and how it is handled.

## Summary

- Bellbound Academy does not require an account to play.
- Bellbound Academy does not show third-party ads.
- Bellbound Academy does not sell personal information.
- Bellbound Academy does not use advertising ID.
- Core gameplay works without creating an online profile.

## Data the game stores on your device

Bellbound Academy stores gameplay settings and local progress on your device so the game can remember things like:

- startup and control preferences
- local simulation settings
- local save or session state
- optional AI dialogue settings

This local data stays on the device unless you choose to back up, export, or sync the device yourself.

## Data the developer does not collect through normal gameplay

During normal gameplay, Bellbound Academy is not designed to collect or transmit to the developer:

- your name
- email address
- precise location
- contacts
- payment card details
- health information
- government ID information
- messages or photos

## Optional AI features

Some builds of Bellbound Academy include optional AI dialogue features. These features are off by default and are intended for local, user-controlled setup.

If you enable an optional AI feature, prompt text generated from gameplay context may be sent to the AI endpoint you configure. In the current release path, this is intended to be a local endpoint controlled by the user. The developer does not receive that content as part of normal gameplay operation.

## Third parties

Bellbound Academy does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Distribution through Google Play may involve standard platform services provided by Google, such as app delivery, crash reporting surfaces inside Play Console, update distribution, and store infrastructure. Your use of Google Play is also subject to Google's own terms and privacy practices.

## Children

Bellbound Academy is not intended for children under 13.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep the app lightweight and local-first. However, no method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure.

## Changes

This policy may be updated if the game adds new online features, account systems, cloud saves, ads, analytics, or other data practices. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

## Contact

For privacy questions about Bellbound Academy, use the support contact published on the app's store listing or project page.
