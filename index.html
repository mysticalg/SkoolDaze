    How this tribute works

    This is an original HTML5 fan tribute inspired by classic school chaos gameplay. It includes bell schedules, lesson routines, personality-driven AI, discipline, and shield objectives.

    • Attend the right room after each bell to avoid lines.
    • Teachers give lesson tasks on blackboards and quiz you.
    • Pupils have personalities (bully, swot, hero, oddball), affecting behaviour.
    • Knockouts can be used tactically to reach difficult shields.
    • Collect all shield letters to reveal the safe code.
