How this tribute works

🎮 Controls

  • 🧭 Move: Arrow Keys / WASD
  • 👊 Punch: Z
  • 🎯 Catapult: X
  • 🚪 Interact: E (doors + stairs)
  • 🪑 Sit / Stand: Q
  • 🥤 Vending: C
  • 🗑️ Throw rubbish: V
  • 🤝 Trade / Barter: T
  • 🚻 Urinals: E (in toilets)
  • 🤖 Auto: U (or leave Eric idle)
  • 🏃 Run: Shift / R + Move
  • ⏯️ Pause: P or top bar button
  • 🧭 Navigation: Follow corridors and use marked stairs between levels. Floor colors: Upper purple, Middle blue, Ground green.

This is an original HTML5 fan tribute inspired by classic school chaos gameplay. It includes bell schedules, lesson routines, personality-driven AI, discipline, and shield objectives.

  • Attend the right room after each bell to avoid lines.
  • Teachers give lesson tasks on blackboards and quiz you.
  • One teacher is on daily break duty while the rest regroup in the staff room.
  • Pupils have personalities (bully, swot, hero, oddball), affecting behaviour.
  • Outside fountains let you hydrate without vending machine rubbish.
  • Toilets can block and flood every 12th day, so plan toilet trips early.
  • Knockouts can be used tactically to reach difficult shields.
  • Collect all shield letters to reveal the safe code.
