How Bellbound Academy works

🎮 Controls

  • 🧭 Move: Arrow Keys / WASD or on-screen touch pad
  • 👊 Punch: Z or Hit
  • 🎯 Catapult: X or Prank
  • 🚪 Interact: E or Use
  • 🪑 Sit / Stand: Q or Sit
  • 🥤 Vending: C or Vend
  • 🗑️ Throw rubbish: V or Throw
  • 🤝 Trade / Barter: T or Trade
  • 🚻 Urinals: E / Use (in toilets)
  • 🤖 Auto: U or Auto
  • 🏃 Run: Shift / R or hold RUN
  • ⏯️ Pause: P or Pause button
  • ⛶ Fullscreen: F or top bar button
  • 🧭 Navigation: Follow corridors and use marked stairs between levels. Floor colors: Upper purple, Middle blue, Ground green.

Bellbound Academy is an original HTML5 school-life sim with bell schedules, lesson routines, social AI, discipline systems, and a full day of structured chaos.

  • Attend the right room after each bell to avoid lines.
  • Teachers give lesson tasks on blackboards and quiz you.
  • One teacher is on daily break duty while the rest regroup in the staff room.
  • Pupils have personalities (bully, swot, hero, oddball), affecting behaviour.
  • Outside fountains let you hydrate without vending machine rubbish.
  • Toilets can block and flood every 12th day, so plan toilet trips early.
  • Knockouts can be used tactically to reach difficult shields.
  • Collect all shield letters to reveal the safe code.

📱 Phones and tablets show a touch pad plus action buttons automatically. The Android shell uses the same game logic and can run offline once installed.
